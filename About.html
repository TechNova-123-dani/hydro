About Us

At Maa Hydro Drilling and Energy Solutions, we specialize in providing sustainable water and energy services that empower communities, businesses, and individuals across Kenya and beyond. We combine advanced technology, modern drilling equipment, and highly trained professionals to deliver projects that stand the test of time. Our expertise covers the entire process — from hydrogeological surveys, borehole drilling, pump testing, and water analysis to pump installations, solar power solutions, and steel tank structures. With years of experience in the industry, we are committed to ensuring that every project is handled with the highest level of precision, efficiency, and professionalism. We believe that access to clean water and reliable energy is not just a service, but a right — and we take pride in making that a reality for our clients. Whether you are a homeowner seeking reliable water supply, a farmer in need of irrigation solutions, or a business looking for a long-term energy backup system, Maa Hydro Drilling and Energy Solutions is your trusted partner. We strive to create solutions that are not only cost-effective but also environmentally friendly, ensuring that the benefits extend to future generations.
